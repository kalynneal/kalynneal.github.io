Senior Seminar

at Golden High School

What is it?

Senior Seminar is a somewhat generic term, so you may be asking yourself what I am talking about. Well, the senior seminar I am refering to is an experiential education program for seniors in high school. Specifically the one I experienced at Golden High School in Golden, CO. It creates an opportunity for students to take their learning outside of the classroom and experience things they normally wouldn't.

Details

Senior Seminar at Golden Highschool was started by Dewain Wood in 1972 and has had a senior class participate in it every year since. It is led by a group of about 3 teachers each year who coordinate the trips/experiences. Students apply for the program and only 25 are chosen based on staff approval, acedemic record, and senior standing. Once the students are chosen, their senior year schedule is changed to fit with the program. The first semester of their senior year is still mostly done in school, but their curriculum and classes are chosen to prepare the students for second semester.

Mission Statement

"Through experiential learning, we cross cultural and classroom boundaries to solve problems, collaborate and communicate within and outside of the group, expanding our school experience to the community and beyond."
