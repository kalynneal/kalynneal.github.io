Introduction to Me!

Text

Hi! My name is Kalyn Neal. I’m 20 years old and am currently a Junior at the University of Northern Colorado studying business management with HR and Digital Marketing certifications.

Background:

I grew up in Golden, Colorado, and graduated from Golden High School in 2019. I love the outdoors, and grew up camping and hiking with my parents, younger sister, and dog. I had many jobs growing up, such as working at an amusement park, nannying, and retail for multiple years. I currently work for JBS at the corporate office here in Greeley. I am a part of the career development program there and am running the Vendor Management department with another intern.

Skills:

I consider myself a pretty well-rounded person and have picked up many skills throughout my life. I am a great listener which helps me focus and be detailed oriented. I am a people person and am very good at handling emotions so I am good at deescalating situations and making people feel at ease. I am a versatile and fast learner which helps me to excel in situations that I may get thrown into abruptly. Coming from my experience with JBS, I am skilled with SAP functions and am able to navigate the program efficiently. Lastly, I am hardworking and once I make a commitment or start a project I am determined to finish it.

What can I do for you?:

If I were to join your team, one of the most important things I would bring to the table is an open mind and new perspectives. I have had many unique experiences which allows me to constantly be thinking from different point of views. I am always open to new opportunities and will never turn down the chance for a new adventure.
