Ted Talks

Humble Plants that Hide Surprising Secrets - Ameenah Gurib-Fakim

Ameenah discusses the values of biodiversity as well as the unique medicinal properties of certain plants. She explains how this knowledge will disapear forever with the destruction of habitats.

How a Team of Chefs Fed Puerto Rico After Hurricane Maria - Jose Andres

Jose talks about his emotional and eye-opening experience resulting from the aftermath of the hurricane. He walks us through how people were able to come togetjer and share resources to provide food to those who truly needed it.

A Teacher Growing Green in the South Bronx - Stephen Ritz

Stephen explains how he is not a gardener or anyone special, just a person who saw a problem and a way to solve it. I truly think that urban agriculture, like what Stephen started, is the future.
