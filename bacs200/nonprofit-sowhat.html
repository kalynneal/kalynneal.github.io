Senior Seminar

at Golden High School

So What?

Benfits/Outcomes

Overall senior seminar provides a way for students to open their horizons and experience things they may never of had the chance to otherwise. It also creates on environment that is more open and accepting, allowing for bonding between students and the other people they meet in the program. Anyone who has been a part of senior seminar will tell you that is is something they will never forget and some of the connections will last a lifetime.
